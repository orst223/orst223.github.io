Alberto National Forest

Alberto National Forest was created by using STRM Tile grabber, merging the DEM files, stretching, clipped, and then saved as a JPG and exported into Blender. From there I created a hillshade of Alberto National Forest in Chile with additions such as roads, borders, water,Glaciers and Park land. I created the color scheme in Adobe Colors using an image of the natural environment of Chile. Then inserting it into Adobe Illustrator where I added titles and names.

Pollution Exposure

Pollution Exposure By Income was designed in hopes of proving that companies such as American Energy Power (AEP) select the locations of plants that cause major air and water pollution by income. AEP was voted the top pollution producing company in the US in 2019 when the map was created. By finding their top plants I took those coordinates and turned them into points, using US census data I was able to display the counties by income in Texas, Kentucky, Ohio, Indiana, Wisconsin, West Virginia, South Carolina, Oklahoma, and Louisiana. I then used a buffer to show how far the pollution could spread and the areas that their air and water could be effected.This map shows that environmental injustice is happening and there is in this case a correlation between pollution producing plant locations and low income areas.
